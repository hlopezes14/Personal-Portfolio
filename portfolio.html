H2U

H2U is an automated in home water recycling system that uses water's pH and EC to determine whether it can reusable by the household. For this research project, I was in charge of the sensor system. That included researching what parts were best for the system, assembling the sensors, and arduino, and calibrating the sensors. I, also, wrote a data retrieval program in C++ for Arduino, that was able to correctly identify and filter water 90% of the time.

Languages/Tools Used:

  • C++
  • Arduino


Red Snow

https://github.com/Daniel-A-Gutierrez/RED-SNOW/
